# HashiHelp Research

This research was conducted to understand the pain points of users living with Hashimoto's Thyroiditis and the challenges they face in tracking their health and symptoms.

## Research Contents
*   **Empathy Research Interviews:** Qualitative data from 1-on-1 user sessions.
*   **User Personas:** Detailed profiles of our target audience based on interview data.
*   **Demographic Data:** A breakdown of empathy research participants.

## Key Goals
* Identify gaps in existing symptom-tracking tools.
* Understand the emotional and physical burden of Hashimoto's management.
* Map the "user journey" from symptom onset to daily management.
